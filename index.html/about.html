About Us

Ar-Raudah Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to improving lives and forecatering sustainable development. Rooted in compassion and community service, we provide critical support to needy and vulnerable groups of individuals including almajiris, children, orphans, women, girls, and persons with disabilities, elderly, and individuals in the community also affected by crises.
